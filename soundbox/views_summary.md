# Orthogonal views of the soundbox

These describe what the four canonical views of the finished soundbox
should look like. Use them as sanity checks on your neck model and the
full assembly.

All dimensions in millimeters.

## Side view (player's view)

Projection along the +z axis (or -z; by symmetry they're identical).

- Shows the full 58° tilted soundboard running from CO (bottom-left)
  diagonally up to ST (top-right)
- Chamber hangs below the soundboard, widest near the middle-bass
- Column is a vertical bar on the left at `x ∈ [12.7, 51.7]`
- Base block fills from top-of-base cap down to floor, wrapping the
  bass-end bulge
- Grommets appear as ~47 small marks along the soundboard line
- Total height: ~1550 mm (from ST at top to floor at bottom)
- Width at base: ~340 mm (column inside face to rightmost bass bulge edge)

## Front view (from column side, looking +x direction)

Projection onto the yz plane.

- Chamber silhouette is a tall teardrop, narrow at top and bottom, widest
  at the middle (~s' = 523)
- Max width ±180 mm in z (width 360 mm)
- Column appears as a narrow vertical strip centered on z = 0
- Because the chamber axis tilts 32° from vertical, the silhouette has
  a slight "sheared" quality relative to vertical (bass end is farther
  right in z than the treble end, by about ±half the chamber depth)

## Back view (from belly side, looking -x direction)

Same silhouette as front view (chamber is symmetric about z = 0). What
differs is what's visible in front vs behind:

- Belly of chamber bulges toward viewer (closer than the soundboard)
- Soundboard and column are "behind" the chamber from this perspective
- Sound holes, if placed, face the viewer
- The 47 grommets on the soundboard are hidden (behind the chamber belly)

## Top view (looking down, -y direction)

Projection onto the xz plane.

- Chamber silhouette is a pear/teardrop shape
- Length: ~964 mm in x (from bass clearance extension at x ≈ -57 to
  treble clearance at x ≈ 907)
- Width: ±180 mm in z (max at the peak near s' = 523, which projects to
  x ≈ 290)
- Column appears as a small square prism on the left (39×39 mm)
- Soundboard axis (grommet line) runs horizontal through the middle at z = 0

## Soundboard-perpendicular view (looking along -n, face-on to soundboard)

Projection onto the (u, z) plane, "face-on" to the tilted soundboard.

- Chamber outline is a pear/teardrop: widest at the peak (s' = 523, D = 360)
- Length: 1726 mm (from bass clearance at s' = -131.59 to treble clearance
  at s' = 1594.86)
- Width: 360 mm max
- 47 grommets on the centerline; C1 at s' = 167.94 (left-ish), G7 at
  s' = 1509.34 (right-ish)
- This is the view most useful for specifying sound hole positions and
  soundboard laminate design
